Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

SnapInbox ("the App") helps users organize screenshots using on-device processing, OCR, categorization, and optional AI-powered enhancements.

Information We Access

The App requires access to screenshots stored on your device in order to:

  • Detect new screenshots
  • Extract text using OCR
  • Organize screenshots into categories
  • Provide search and filtering features
  • Generate titles, summaries, and tags

Data Storage

Screenshot information, extracted text, categories, and related metadata are stored locally on your device. We do not sell your personal information.

AI Features

Optional AI enhancement features may send screenshot text (OCR text only) to third-party AI providers to generate improved titles, summaries, and tags. Images themselves are not transmitted for AI enhancement unless explicitly stated in a future update.

Advertising

The App may display rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect information according to its own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Gemini (optional AI features)

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Your Choices

You may revoke screenshot access permissions at any time through your device settings.
