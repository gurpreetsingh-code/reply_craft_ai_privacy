LandSnap Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

LandSnap provides tools for measuring land, property, area, distance, perimeter, GPS paths, and camera-assisted measurements.

Permissions Used

  • Location (Walk Around Measurement)
  • Camera (Camera AR Beta)
  • Internet Access

Measurement Data

Measurements, reports, and saved records are stored locally on the user's device.

Analytics & Diagnostics

LandSnap uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics for performance monitoring and crash reporting.

Advertising

LandSnap may display advertisements using Google AdMob.

Maps

LandSnap may use OpenStreetMap and Google Maps services depending on the selected map mode.

Data Security

We do not sell personal information. Reasonable safeguards are used to protect data.

Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
