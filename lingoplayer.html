Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

LingoPlayer respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information.

Information We Collect

LingoPlayer primarily stores learning data locally on your device, including:

  • Saved vocabulary
  • Saved sentences
  • Review history
  • Learning statistics

This information remains on your device unless you choose to export or back up your data.

Advertising

LingoPlayer may display rewarded advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

The app may use analytics and crash reporting services to improve performance and stability.

Data Storage

Your learning data is stored locally on your device. LingoPlayer does not upload your vocabulary, review history, or saved sentences to external servers.

Permissions

The app may request permissions such as:

  • Storage access (to access videos and subtitles)
  • Media access

Permissions are used only for app functionality.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

Children's Privacy

LingoPlayer is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
